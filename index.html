About Me

I am one of the co-founder of Genieland. Genieland Company Limited is an Artificial Intelligence and IOT based startup company, whom aim to create a smart assistant to help and encourage people to practice various of sport anywhere at any time. In the meantime, I also provide professional consultant service on Computer Vision related projects to different scale and industry companies. Apart from commercial work, I also contribute my time on education side. Currently, I am a supervisor of two groups of student from Higher Diploma in Data Science and Analytics from Institute of Vocational Education, mentoring them on their Final Year Project. I am always delighted to share my experience, no matter on the consulting or academic projects, for more details, feel free to contact me directly.

Previously, I was an Undergraduate from the University of Birmingham. At that time, I worked with Anh Pham, Mayang Parahita, Mathias Chaouche, and James Rees on "Efficient People-Searching Robot" which published by TAROS in 2016. For my final year project, I worked on Leap Motion, which is a real-time gesture detecting device. I visualise them with Visualisation ToolKits (VTK), and create a gesture interface for 3D model viewing. These projects make me started to be fascinate on Computer Vision and further now on Deep Learning. In particular, I am experienced on Face/Object/Gesture Recognition Algorithms, including various Convolutional Neural Networks architectures. Yet, my latest focus was on Human Pose Estimation and Action Recognition, which makes me end up creating Genieland with my other partners.

Similar to other nerds, I am always inspired by movies and also lots of sci-fi stuff. However, looking through the "AI Hype", there's still a lot of room for even state-of-the-art AI to achieve human-level equivalent intelligence. Feel free to chat with me on any related topics, I wish my work could contribute to fulfilling this Ultimate Goal.
